Legal statements

Via phone

  1. Call +33 806 20 30 40 (local call rates apply)
  2. Have a credit card ready and the payment form on the received notice.
  3. Let yourself be guided by the French spoken instructions

You will need to fill in:
  1. The e-payment number and key: you will find them on the form or the payment slip
  2. Your credit card number and expiry date
  3. The CVV number on the credit card: the last 3 digits that appear on the back of your card near the signature.


It is possible to obtain a proof of payment if requested.

Via mobile app

  1. Download the « amendes.gouv » application on Google Play or the App Store
  2. Have a credit card ready and the payment form on the received notice.
  3. Scan the code printed on your notice.
You will need to fill in:
  1. Your credit card number and expiry date
  2. The CVV number on the credit card: the last 3 digits that appear on the back of your card near the signature.

If your traffic offence notice or your fine notice does not include a QR code at the bottom left of the payment slip, payment is also possible by entering the e-payment number and its key: you will find them on the form or the payment slip.
